About Me

A bit more about
who I am

I'm Krishna — a self-taught developer who started coding because I was too curious about how websites actually work. That curiosity turned into a full-blown obsession, and now I spend my days building things with C#, Python, Django, and whatever tool gets the job done right.

I'm not the kind of person who memorizes documentation for fun (well, maybe a little). I'm the kind who sees a problem, gets slightly annoyed, and then builds something to solve it. I believe clean code is not just about syntax — it's about respect for the next person who reads it.

When I'm not coding, I'm probably watching tech videos at 2AM, sketching UI ideas, or convincing myself that "one more tutorial" will make me a 10x developer.
